Who We Are

Engineering Excellence on Every Road

RoadDoctor is a leading civil and road engineering company headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria. We combine deep technical expertise with practical on-ground experience to deliver road infrastructure that withstands Nigeria's challenging climate and traffic conditions.

From federal highways to urban access roads, we apply international best practices — grounded in AASHTO, BS, and Nigerian Highway Manual standards — to every kilometre we build, rehabilitate, or maintain.

Compliance & Standards

Codes & Standards We Work By

Nigeria

Federal Highway Manual

Full compliance with the Nigerian Federal Highway Manual — design speeds, lane widths, sight distances, and superelevation standards for all road classifications.

AASHTO

AASHTO Pavement Design

AASHTO 1993 Mechanistic-Empirical Pavement Design Guide for structural pavement thickness calculations based on traffic loading (ESALs) and soil resilient modulus.

British

BS & HD Standards

Highways England Design Manuals (HD) and British Standards for drainage, geotechnical work, materials specification, and bridge structural design.

Testing

ASTM & NIS Testing

All material testing — aggregate gradation, Proctor compaction, CBR, Marshall stability, concrete strength — carried out to ASTM and Nigerian Industrial Standards (NIS).

Safety

iRAP Road Safety Standards

International Road Assessment Programme (iRAP) protocols applied in road safety audits to systematically identify and eliminate life-threatening infrastructure deficiencies.

Environment

NESREA Regulations

Environmental compliance with National Environmental Standards and Regulations Enforcement Agency (NESREA) guidelines throughout all phases of project delivery.
